Welcome to Lethbridge Tennis Club!

The Lethbridge Tennis Club (LTC), established in 1887, is Alberta’s longest-running tennis club. Located at 2611 Parkside Drive South near Spitz Stadium, LTC offers six tennis courts, a ball machine, viewing deck, night lights, clubhouse, washrooms, kitchen, and BBQ area - perfect for lessons, social events, and play. Everyone, from beginners to seasoned players, is welcome to join and be part of the LTC family!
