Welcome to Lethbridge Tennis Club!

We are located at 2611 Parkside Drive South, just east of Spitz Stadium. Parking is available via 6 Ave. S. at 25 Street S.

LTC has 6 tennis courts, coaching for all levels, and social events. Everyone is welcome, from beginners to experienced players.

Come play tennis or join our community events.
